师父领进门，修行在个人
teachers open the doors,

To begin to solve the problems in your life, you must first be ready to accept this truth: no one can solve your problems for you.


授人以魚不如授人以漁
give a man a fish and you feed him for a day;
teach a man to fish and you feed him for a lifetime

We help you understand your problem: where it comes from, its nature, how to solve it, how it relates to your identity, and how it relates to the larger context of the human experience.


"And ye shall know the truth,
and the truth shall make you free."

Through understanding, you will solve your own problem.
