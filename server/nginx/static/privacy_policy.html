Information Collection and Use

Basic Account Information
If you choose to create a Shifu Life Consultants account, you must provide us with some personal information, such as your name, username, password, and email address. On Shifu Life Consultants, your username is not listed publicly. You can create and manage multiple Shifu Life Consultants accounts.

Contact Information
You may use your email address and WeChat as your contact information. We may use your contact information to send you requests for more information regarding your application essays, and send you the application essays that we help you write.

Additional Information
You must provide us with additional information to help write your application essays. For example, you must answer the required questions in the survey so that we can help you write your academic background in your Personal Statement. We will store the information you provide in the Survey till we complete the Services. If you email us, we may keep your message, email address, and contact information to respond to your request.

Using Our Services

This Log Data may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, the referring web page, pages visited, location, your mobile carrier, device information (including device and application IDs), search terms, or cookie information. We also receive Log Data when you click on, view or interact with links on our Services. We use Log Data to make inferences, like what Services you may be interested in. We keep Log Data as needed for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. We will either delete Log Data or remove any common account identifiers, such as your username, full IP address, email address, or phone number, after a maximum of 18 months, if not sooner.
